IELTS Listening Test 1

Important Instructions

  • The test contains 4 Sections, each with 10 questions.
  • Once the test begins, the audio will play without pause.
  • You cannot replay or go back to previous sections.
  • Before each audio starts, you will have 5 seconds to read the questions.
  • After Section 4 ends, you will have 2 minutes to check your answers.
  • Your final score will be shown automatically.
